PHILOSOPHY

Every child has innate capabilities that help him/her shine. Our expert faculty provides the right guidance to bring out these latent qualities, transforming the student into a successful individual.Sri Chaitanya Syllabus is a unique one with an orientation of enabling the students take up to the task of getting selected for the Prestigious IIT’s, NIIT’S, AIIMS, JIPMER, AIPMD, DEEMED UNIVERSITIES and other Medical and Engineering Institutes and other streams with ease and comfort.

CURRICULUM

Sri Chaitanya devised a strong Curriculum, integrating the components of Maths, Physics, Chemistry and Biology which are needed for the various Entrance Exams under the name of TECHNO CURRICULUM.

The Content Matter is divided into optimum doses so that the students do not feel overburdened with the syllabus. At the same time care is taken to see that the Components of the Techno Curriculum are strictly according to their Normal Course of Study related to the Class in which they are studying at present.

This makes them study their present syllabus in depth with the help of the Micro Level Teaching, providing them the chance of improving their performance in the current Class in which they are studying.


C-BATCH:

Our C-Olympiad batch is a platform where the students with a strong academics potential and inclination are trained on customized preparatory material to take on the tough National and International Olympiads and step into the portals of prestigious National level professional institutions-IIT’s, NIT’s etc.

The Main Objective of C-Batch Programming is to see that the IIT’s all over the country are filled with the majority of Sri Chaitanya School Students. To see Sri Chaitanya Students in the Majority in the TOP – 100 is the Primary Objective


MEDICON:

To see Sri Chaitanya Students in the Majority in the TOP-100 of all the Prestigious Medical Institutions is the Primary Objective of the Medicon Programme.

Medicon - Batch is a specially designed programme, preparing the students to appear for the state and National Medical Entrance Examinations. It also enables them to secure seats in AIMS, JIPMER, NEET etc. and top ranks both in the National and State level.


IPL-BATCH:

The IPL Batch is created with the sole aim of seeing that the majority of TOP 10 & TOP – 50 Students for IIT Selections are filled with the students from Sri Chaitanya Schools.

Contemporary dimensions to IIT-JEEE, concept based preparatory course materials in Mathhe students to face the IIT level entrance exams. IPL batch is a sure bet to secure success in any national and international level exams. At SCTS-IPL we just don’t prepare them to write IIT entrance exams but prepare them to write any exams in the IIT level competence.


MPL-BATCH:

The MPL Batch is created with the sole aim of seeing that the majority of TOP 10 & TOP – 50 Students selected for all the Prestigious Medical Institutions like JIPMER, NEET, AIIMS and AIPMT are filled with the students from Sri Chaitanya Schools.

Just on the same lines like IPL, MPL is meant for a sure success in all the National Level examinations in the Medical Arena, like JIPMER, NEET, AIPMT and AIIMS. Those who have medicine as their dream project in their life can find MPL Programme a sure and definite means of cracking it.


THE OLYMPIADS:

Today’s world characterizes success to the endurance for competition.

This needs a lot of practice. Those who study for a long twelve year period and complete Intermediate education with the best credentials also cannot come out successful through exams like EAMCET, NEET, IIT & CIVILS.

Hence all the students are made to take up many Competitive Exams and Olympiads.


CIVILS BATCH:

We are glad to inform that the CIVILS BATCH has been added to the Sri Chaitanya Curriculum from the year 2018-19 onwards.

This is mainly aimed at coaching the students who opt this Programme achieve a Civils Rank, a Group-1 or Group-2 Job or a High Profile job in any National Level Body like Railways, Banking or Insurance Sectors.

The Programme highlights the need for the knowledge of Social Sciences, Mathematics with Reasoning, Communication Skills, General Awareness and Current Affairs. Highly Potential Material in all these areas will be provided for the Study and Practice of the students.Regular Descriptive and Objective Tests, to ensure sufficient practice, will be conducted. Faculty with tons of experience to lead the students of this batch to Success are provided at all the Branches where the Course is offered.
